Managerial Code of Conduct

As a manager, you are expected to adhere to the following code of conduct:

  • Leadership: Lead by example and inspire your team.
  • Integrity: Uphold high ethical standards and honesty in all actions.
  • Communication: Foster open and transparent communication.
  • Respect: Treat all team members and stakeholders with respect.
  • Professionalism: Maintain professionalism in all interactions.
  • Growth: Support the growth and development of your team members.
  • Compliance: Ensure compliance with company policies and regulations.
  • Conflict Resolution: Effectively manage conflicts and promote resolution.
  • Feedback: Provide constructive feedback and encourage continuous improvement.
